How people are coming up with ideas? Somehow, from some where they are getting the spark. But the conscious pickup of concepts, contexts, knowledge or experience affect the unconscious decisions we make every day.

Design decisions made by a designer largely depends on constraints. As designer grasp more constraints affecting the design problem, the design decisions becomes more effective (of course skill of the designer is a major factor).
